**How to use these notes**

These notes follow the course module by module. Each module page gives
you the outcome (what you'll be able to do), the key points to remember,
a diagram or space for a photo, and room for your own notes. Write in
them freely — the best instructors keep a working notebook.

The notes mirror the Teaching System Handbook (the standard) and the
Instructor Certification Course Guide (the course). Where the two go
deeper, this workbook points you there.

> **You will be able to:** explain where you fit in the BADGES system
> and why the separation of instructing and examining exists.

**Key points**

> **•** MAS runs BADGES: the Board, the Coaching & Technical Board, the
> Chairperson, the Chief Examiner, and the Master Trainer.
>
> **•** Six governing documents: the Manual, the 7-Level Syllabus, the
> Teaching System Handbook, the Assessment System Handbook, and the two
> Course Guides.
>
> **•** The certificate certifies the swimmer, never the coach.
>
> **•** The one rule: every assessment is booked by a certified
> instructor, and whoever assesses a swimmer is never that swimmer's own
> teacher.

**The Seven Levels — Teaching Guide**

This section is your level-by-level companion. For each of the seven
levels you'll find the teaching objectives (what to Introduce,
Reinforce, and Develop), the full assessment criteria your swimmers must
meet, and the key correction cues for the faults most common at that
stage.

Use these pages in lessons: before a session, remind yourself what the
level demands; during, look up the cue for whatever you're seeing;
after, note what's progressing and what still needs work.

**Pathway overview**

|                        |                             |                                                                                                                                                    |
|------------------------|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Phase**              | **Levels**                  | **Focus**                                                                                                                                          |
| **Foundation**         | L1–L4 · Starfish to Octopus | Water confidence, safe entry/exit, basic strokes (FC, BK), survival skills, body position, kicking foundations, bilateral breathing, basic diving. |
| **Stroke Development** | L5–L6 · Frog & Swordfish    | All four competitive strokes, sculling, flip and open turns, stand dive, distance and efficiency.                                                  |
| **Competitive Bridge** | L7 · Dolphin                | High-elbow FC, competitive BK pull, IM 100m, time qualifications — competition foundation.                                                         |

**Key correction cues for this level**

|                                             |                                     |                                                                 |
|---------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
| **FAULT**                                   | **LIKELY CAUSE**                    | **CUES YOU CAN USE**                                            |
| **Front/back float — hips sink**            | Head lifted · empty lungs · tension | "Look down, belly to the sky, big lungs; ears in on your back." |
| **Streamline — arms apart, quick sink**     | No lock position                    | "Squeeze your ears, hide your chin, point like an arrow."       |
| **Flutter kick — big knee bend, no travel** | Kicking from the knee, stiff ankles | "Long legs, floppy feet, kick from the hips — small and fast."  |
